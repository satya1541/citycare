Anti-Discrimination Policy

City Expert Care

City Expert Care seeks to empower millions of service professionals to deliver safe, reliable, and high-quality services at home.

Our Commitment

City Expert Care does not tolerate, and prohibits discrimination against customers or service providers based on any of the following protected characteristics:

Religion Caste Race National Origin Disability Sexual Orientation Sex Marital Status Gender Identity Age

This policy extends to any other characteristic that may be protected under applicable laws.

Prohibited Conduct

Such discrimination includes, but is not limited to, refusing to provide or accept services based on any of the protected characteristics mentioned above.

⚠️

Any customer or service partner found to have violated this prohibition will lose access to the City Expert Care platform.
